G79 Auto Officially Opens Its First Showroom in Hanoi

On June 14, 2025, G79 Auto – a premium automotive company specializing in luxury car trading, maintenance, customization, refurbishment, and high-end rental services – officially inaugurated its first showroom at 66–68 Nguyen Van Cu, Long Bien, Hanoi.

Located in the heart of the capital, G79 Auto offers a refined experience with top-tier brands such as Porsche, Volvo, and BMW, alongside other prestigious luxury models. Each vehicle undergoes a rigorous quality inspection and transparent history verification process, ensuring superior performance and complete peace of mind for every customer.

The grand opening ceremony was attended by the founding team, valued clients, and strategic partners from the automotive and lifestyle sectors, as well as representatives and congratulations from leading banks including ACB, VietinBank, BIDV, and Vietcombank — a testament to G79 Auto’s solid reputation and strong financial foundation. We deeply appreciate the beautiful floral tributes and heartfelt wishes from our friends and partners who have accompanied G79 Auto from its earliest concept to its realization.

G79 Auto is more than a destination for luxury car transactions — it is a trusted companion that helps customers express their lifestyle and individuality. With a team of professional, dedicated, and honest consultants, we are committed to placing customer satisfaction and trust at the heart of everything we do.

To mark this milestone, G79 Auto also unveiled a bespoke perfume collection inspired by five iconic Nordic destinations — from the deep blue fjords of Lofoten, the white nights of Stockholm, and the summer forests of Dalarna, to the mythical mists of Geiranger and the majestic solitude of Nordkapp. Each fragrance is a sensory map, guiding you toward moments of authenticity, serenity, and emotional depth.

With its “Luxury and Compact” model — refined yet efficient, elegant yet distinctive — G79 Auto embodies its philosophy of delivering streamlined luxury, tailored to diverse spaces and personalities.
