GG CORPORATION – Main Sponsor of the Concert “Khát Vọng” (Aspiration)

“Khát Vọng” (Aspiration) — a special concert held at Ho Guom Opera House, where the delicate melodies of composer Phú Quang once again resonated, awakening emotions, memories, and a deep love for Hanoi in the hearts of the audience.

As the Main Sponsor of the concert, GG CORPORATION takes pride in accompanying and supporting genuine artistic values — where music, emotion, and humanity harmonize to celebrate the beauty of Vietnamese culture.

With a mission to cultivate a comprehensive lifestyle standard and deliver premium wellbeing experiences, GG CORPORATION not only provides world-class products and services but also pioneers in supporting meaningful cultural and artistic initiatives — spreading the spirit of “Live with Class – Live with Emotion.”

Under the GG CORPORATION umbrella, member brands such as G79 AUTO and GG YOUNG share a common vision: to enhance the quality of life and affirm Vietnamese values on an international stage.

GG CORPORATION extends its heartfelt appreciation to the organizers, artists, and audience for contributing to a truly emotional night of “Khát Vọng” — where artistic excellence and humanistic values were elevated in harmony.

Venue: Ho Guom Opera House
Time: 8:00 PM, October 4, 2025

Artistic Director: Trinh Huong
Produced by: Phu Quang Music & Arts
Editor: Giang Huong
Photography: Phu Vuong
Orchestra: Hanoi Symphony Orchestra
Conductor: Tran Nhat Minh
Violin: Bui Cong Duy
Saxophone: Tran Manh Tuan
Piano: Minh Tuan, Trinh Huong
Performers: Thanh Lam, Tung Duong, Ngoc Anh, Dao Mac, and Tuan Hiep
