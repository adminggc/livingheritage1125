Jazz Legend Niels Lan Doky Returns to Vietnam for the Living Heritage – Jazz Concert IMMERSED

GG Corporation officially introduces Living Heritage (Heritage for the Future), with a special highlight this year — Jazz Concert IMMERSED, taking place on November 15, 2025, at GEM Center, Ho Chi Minh City.

Living Heritage — Timeless Values for Future Generations

Living Heritage is a cultural initiative honoring the humanistic values, wisdom, and spirit preserved and passed down by outstanding Vietnamese individuals around the world. More than a tribute to Vietnamese excellence, the project seeks to create a space that connects generations and inspires a lifestyle of elegance, kindness, humility, and strength.

Ms. Luu Bao Huong, Chairwoman of GG Corporation, shared: “After years of developing Premium Wellbeing solutions, GG Corporation believes that sustainable quality of life is not only built on material success, but also on the elevation of the soul, love, and the pursuit of truth, goodness, and beauty. Through Living Heritage, we hope to create a space for connection and the sharing of meaningful Vietnamese values — an invaluable source of wisdom and inspiration for a better, more sustainable, and happier future.”

The Living Heritage ecosystem includes:

  • Website: www.livingheritage.vn — a hub of mindful learning and in-depth resources about inspiring Vietnamese figures;
  • E-book: Living Heritage – The Universal Within, launching at the Living Heritage Exhibition in November 2025 in Ho Chi Minh City and distributed globally on Amazon in both English and Vietnamese;
  • Annual international art event: in 2025 represented by Jazz Concert IMMERSED, where heritage is expressed through pure, sophisticated, and emotionally resonant musical performance.

Jazz Concert IMMERSED — The “Return to Roots” Journey of a World-Class Artist

Jazz, with its improvisation, depth, and freedom of expression, is regarded as a universal musical flow — reflecting the refined taste of those who appreciate art. Every swing rhythm, every harmonic nuance, every breath of the musician weaves together to create an immersive experience — where listeners find not only beauty, but also tranquility and inspiration within themselves.

True to its name, Jazz Concert IMMERSED is designed to let the audience dive deep into music and emotion. Inspired by the concept of “Organic Flow” — the Pure Flow — the concert unfolds like the circle of life: continuous, infinite, and elevating the listener with each turn toward deeper, more refined experiences.

At its heart is Sir Niels Lan Doky, the Vietnamese-born Danish jazz pianist and “Knight of Jazz.” Recognized as a pioneer of modern European jazz, Doky has captivated audiences at Carnegie Hall (USA), Royal Albert Hall (UK), Blue Note (Tokyo), and numerous international festivals. Japanese media praised him as “the finest artist of his generation,” while Spain’s El Diario called him “one of the most important jazz pianists of the past half-century.”

In 2010, Niels Lan Doky was knighted by the Queen of Denmark for his exceptional contributions to international music. His return to Vietnam after nearly two decades marks not only an artistic milestone but also a profound journey back to his roots.

Performing as a trio with Felix Pastorius (bass, USA) and Jonas Johansen (drums, Denmark), Doky will take audiences through the full spectrum of contemporary jazz — dynamic yet deeply romantic.

A highlight of the evening will be his collaboration with Vietnam’s top artists, including People’s Artist Thanh Lam, Ha Tran, and saxophonist Quyen Thien Dac — musicians who have preserved the soul of Vietnamese music while continuously innovating to connect with global audiences.

This remarkable encounter promises a multidimensional musical dialogue — where the global jazz spirit meets the essence of contemporary Vietnamese sounds in perfect harmony and spontaneity.

World-Class Creative Team Behind an Immersive Experience

To craft a concert that engages not only the ear but all senses, Jazz Concert IMMERSED brings together exceptional international artists and a top-tier creative team.

Pham Hoang Nam, General Director of the show, said: “For me, the stage is not just a place to perform — it’s a place to tell stories. A world-class concert requires not only technical excellence and international-standard production, but also emotional artistry. Jazz Concert IMMERSED is the meeting point of two creative forces: international musical thinking and Vietnamese soul. The result is a harmonious ‘musical dialogue’ — unexpected yet natural, powerful yet poetic.”

As Music Director, composer Quoc Trung joins Niels Lan Doky in blending Vietnamese traditional melodies with contemporary global sounds, brought to life by a lineup of talented, distinctive artists — promising an unforgettable night.

Adding another creative layer, all performance costumes will be designed by Tom Trandt, a Parsons School of Design graduate whose collections have graced major fashion runways. Recently, one of his designs was chosen for Meryl Streep in the upcoming The Devil Wears Prada 2. His focus on sustainability and recycled materials aligns perfectly with Living Heritage’s mission — toward a more humane and sustainable Vietnam.
