A Joyful Mid-Autumn Gathering – Celebrating the G79 Auto Family Spirit

At G79 Auto, we believe our workplace is more than just a company — it is a family, where every member belongs, shares joy, and spreads positive energy together.

Recently, G79 Auto hosted the “Mid-Autumn Family Gathering”, a heartwarming celebration for the children and families of our staff. The evening was filled with laughter, glowing lanterns, cheerful music, and thoughtful gifts — creating a festive atmosphere that brought smiles to everyone’s faces.

The celebration became even more memorable when the entire G79 Auto family joined together to celebrate the first birthday of little Pepsi, our adorable youngest member. It was a truly sweet and loving moment, capturing the spirit of togetherness that defines who we are.

For G79 Auto, occasions like this are not just about celebration — they are part of our ongoing journey to nurture a caring corporate culture, where people connect, grow, and share happiness as one.

We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to all G79 Auto team members and their families for making this Mid-Autumn Festival such a joyful and memorable one!
