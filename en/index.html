Living Heritage – A Legacy of Values for Future Generations

GG Corporation proudly introduces Living Heritage, a visionary initiative dedicated to gathering and celebrating the authentic life values of the global Vietnamese community—from experiences and knowledge to the profound philosophies of generations past, to be preserved and passed on to the generations of the future.

The project will unfold as a comprehensive multimedia platform, encompassing:

  • Website www.livingheritage.vn – A self-learning platform devoted to cultivating a quality “space for the mind,” featuring in-depth materials and inspiring stories of influential Vietnamese figures from around the world.
  • The Annual E-book Series “Living Heritage” – The first volume, “The Universal Within – The World of the Mind,” is scheduled to debut at the Living Heritage Exhibition in November 2025 in Ho Chi Minh City and will be distributed globally on Amazon.
  • Annual International Artistic Events – Each year, a world-class artistic experience will embody the spirit of Living Heritage. The inaugural event, Concert IMMERSED, embraces the philosophy of Edutainment—education through art and emotion—creating a space where music becomes both an enlightening and profoundly inspiring experience.

LEGACY FOR THE FUTURE

Living Heritage is an online platform within the GG Corporation ecosystem, dedicated to preserving and celebrating the finest values of Vietnamese culture through art, knowledge, and lifestyle.

Inspired by the philosophy “Time & Space as the New Luxury,” the website offers a journey that bridges tradition and modernity, stillness and creativity. It is a place where one can find inspiration to live more slowly, deeply, and gracefully—through art projects, human stories, digital publications, and the annual IMMERSED Jazz Concert.

More than a storytelling space, Living Heritage is where values continue to breathe, to be shared, and to be reborn in the rhythm of contemporary Vietnamese life.

Each featured individual represents a distinct facet of their inner universe. The Universal Within portrays how they live, create, and inspire in their own unique ways—yet all share a common essence: being globally influential Vietnamese figures.

Each year, this journey will honor ten exemplary individuals whose refined, humble way of life embodies meaningful influence and contribution—those with profound inner worlds who quietly dedicate themselves to their craft and to life with a loving heart.
